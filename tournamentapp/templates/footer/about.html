Football Tournament Manager – Round-Robin Tournament Software

Football Tournament Manager is an online platform built for organizing short-format football tournaments across multiple fields. Automate scheduling, manage teams, enter match scores, and track standings — all with secure Google login and no manual registration required.

Secure Google Login – No Account Registration Required

Sign in instantly using Google OAuth authentication. There’s no need to create or remember a new account. Our secure login system allows organizers to access their tournaments quickly and safely.

Automated Round-Robin Scheduling

Instantly generate a complete round-robin schedule for your tournament. Ideal for school competitions, local leagues, 5-a-side tournaments, and short one-half matches. Ensure balanced fixtures while optimizing match flow across multiple fields.

Custom Match & Break Duration

Configure custom game length and pause duration between matches to match your event format. Perfect for fast-paced tournaments and single-half games.

Multi-Field Tournament Management

Assign matches to different fields and manage simultaneous games efficiently. Designed for tournaments running multiple matches at the same time.

Score Entry & Automatic Standings

Enter match results after each game and let the system automatically calculate:

  • Points and rankings
  • Wins, draws, and losses
  • Goal difference
  • Top scorers

Substitution & Card Tracking

Track substitutions, yellow cards, and red cards per team to maintain accurate discipline records throughout the tournament.

Sponsor Banners & Dedicated Tournament Link

Add styled sponsor banners to provide visibility for partners and local businesses. Each tournament includes a unique shareable link so teams, players, and supporters can easily access schedules and standings.
