How to Add Teams and Players

You can add teams in two ways:

  1. Type or paste team names in the textarea. Use a dash (-) to add players below a team.
  2. Upload a CSV file with the same format.

Example format:

Team A
- Player 1
- Player 2

Team B
- Player 3
- Player 4
